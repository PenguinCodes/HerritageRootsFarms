## Our Mission

Heritage Roots Desi Goats and Baby Goats are sourced from indigenous goat breeds, including Betal, Sirohi, and well-adapted local breeds, raised in open and natural environments. These animals are nurtured using time-tested, village-based farming practices that emphasize free grazing, humane care, and respect for natural growth cycles.

## Key characteristics of Heritage Roots Desi Goats and Baby Goats

### Authentic Native Breeds :

They come exclusively from recognized Desi goat breeds such as Betal and Sirohi, along with resilient local breeds known for their adaptability and hardiness.

### Naturally Raised :

Both goats and baby goats are raised without synthetic growth enhancers or routine chemical interventions, following clean and traditional rearing methods.

### Healthy Growth and Development :

Adult goats benefit from natural grazing and open movement, while baby goats grow alongside their mothers, supporting strong immunity and balanced physical development.

### Farmed with Care :

Each goat and baby goat reflects a commitment to ethical livestock practices, supporting small-scale farmers and preserving sustainable rural farming traditions.
